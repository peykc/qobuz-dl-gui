QobuzDL

Connect to Qobuz

Qobuz no longer supports password login. Use OAuth (recommended) or paste your token directly.

Opens Qobuz in your browser. Log in, then the app captures the token automatically.

After login, the status dot will turn green automatically.

Paste your user_id and user_auth_token from Qobuz web player localStorage (F12 → Application → Local Storage → https://play.qobuz.com).

Email/password login is deprecated by Qobuz and may not work. Try OAuth first.
